This lovely hotel located in Pokhara provides free breakfast and have a beautiful mountain view. There is nothing more gorgeous than stunning views of the Annapurna Himalayas in the morning. It also has free wifi for custimer, which is very rare in Nepal. The cost for a night at the hotel Barahi is only $62.
