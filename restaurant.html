About The Atithi Satkar Restaurant

Atithi Restaurant is a small family and friends run business in Nepal. They have branches of this restaurant all over the country, like Jyatha, Thamel, Kathmandu and more. The prices of food might be a little expensive however it is worth it because they prepare each dish with fresh ingredients, low fat and significant reduction in-food coloring will have a fantastic atmosphere and great foods with Nepalese cultural dance. They also large selection when it comes to dishes, they serve continental dishes as well. If you ever go to Nepal, check this place out in your stay in Nepal because not only you get to eat delicious Nepalese food but also experience and gain more about Nepali cultures since they have events every day.
